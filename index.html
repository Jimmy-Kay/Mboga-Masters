    About Us

    We Care What You Eat!

    Mboga Masters is a food store located on Mombasa Road inside TOTALENERGIES Syokimau. We are Kenya’s first exclusive food store and have been operational since September 2013.

    Mboga Masters began as a green grocery, selling fresh fruits and vegetables. We do office & home deliveries along Mombasa Road stretching from South B/C areas all the way to Kitengela & Athi River.

    As our slogan suggests (‘We Care What You Eat!’), our aim was and continues to be not only to sell high quality produce, but also to educate our customers by giving them useful nutrition tips and recipes in-store, through our social media platforms.

    We stock a wide variety of fresh fruits and vegetables, herbs, spices and cereals and food related groceries.
    Our butchery offers the best cuts of fish, broiler & kienyeji chicken, beef, steak, minced beef, lamb and goat.
    We also have a restaurant and corporate catering service that offers tasty, freshly made food and snacks with a variety of healthy alternatives such as salads, juices and smoothies.

    We have the capacity and experience to promise you meals that are:
    1. NUTRITIONALLY BALANCED
    2. TASTY
    3. CONSISTENT IN QUALITY
    4. HYGIENICALLY PREPARED

    Why choose us

    Traceablility

    As much as possible, we ensure that we can trace both our fresh produce and meats to the farm. We also ensure that the food we stock is either 100% organic or safely grown.

    Variety

    We stock a variety of fresh fruits & vegetables ensuring that you can conveniently shop from one location for all your food-related needs.

    Holistic Approach

    Healthy living is a lot more than about eating well. We encourage healthy lifestyles through sharing nutritional information with customers, through regular fitness events, organic markets, and cooking demonstrations that help customers make healthier choices.
